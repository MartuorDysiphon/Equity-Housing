ABOUT US

The Best Student Life Starts Here!

Equity Housing is dedicated to providing students with a safe and comfortable living environment that feels like home. Our mission is to create a supportive community where students can thrive academically and socially, while enjoying modern amenities and hassle-free living.

Founded by passionate individuals who understand the needs of students, our focus is on creating secure environments that support academic success and personal growth. We work closely with institutions like University of Free State & Central University Of Technology and partners to keep our standards high and our service reliable.
